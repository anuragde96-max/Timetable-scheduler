How to Use SchedulePro

Follow these steps to generate optimal schedules for your workplace:

Step 1: Download the Template

Click the Download Template button in the sidebar to get a template Excel sheet ready with correct formatting.

Step 2: Add your Constraints

  • Settings: Set the planning period dates and standard shift hours.
  • Resources: List your staff members/doctors, their total target hours, and specific days off (comma-separated).
  • Departments: Define the work zones/courses and total minimum/maximum coverage hours required.
  • Mappings: Specify which staff are allowed in which departments, including department-specific min/max days limit.

Step 3: Upload and Process

Upload the template. The app will validate your data and display errors if any dates are invalid or columns are missing.

Step 4: Solve and Inspect

Click Generate Schedule. The system will search for a fully valid timetable. If one isn't possible, it outputs a best-match schedule with highlighted warnings detailing exactly which constraints had to be relaxed.
